Husky or Malamute?

Can you create a project that can identify a husky and a malamute? They look very similar, so train your classifier well! Put your Train AI keys into the blocks in codelab to check them out.

Project Guide

  • Step 1: Download the project. If you have not trained an image project that can huskies or malamutes, you can use this data and train a new model. Use the categories "husky" and "malamute".

  • Step 2: After training your model, write down your API key and the model name to use in the starter project you downloaded.
